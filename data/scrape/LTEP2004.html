Political parties represented in the election:

  • DP - Labour Party (Darbo partija)
  • LSP - Lithuanian Social Democratic Party (Lietuvos socialdemokratu partija)
  • TS - Homeland Union (Conservatives, Political Prisoners and Deportees, Christian Democrats)    (Tevynes sajunga)
  • LCS - Liberal and Centre Union (Liberalu ir centro sajunga)
  • VNDPS - Union of Farmers' Party and New Democracy Party (Valstieciu ir Naujosios demokratijos partiju sajunga)
  • LDP - Liberal Democratic Party (Liberalu demokratu partija)
  • KmS - "Together we are strong" - Coalition between the Electoral Action of Lithuania's Poles (LLRA) and Lithuanian Russian Union (LRS) (Lietuvos lenku rinkimu akcijos ir Lietuvos rusu sajungos koalicija „Kartu mes jega!“)
  • NS - The New Union (Social Liberals) (Naujoji sajunga (socialliberalai))
  • LKD - Lithuanian Christian Democrats (Lietuvos krikcionys demokratai)
  • KKSS - Christian Conservative Social Union (Krikcioniu konservatoriu socialine sajunga)
  • TPP - The Party of national Progress (Tautos paangos partija)
  • NCP - National Centre Party (Nacionaline centro partija)
