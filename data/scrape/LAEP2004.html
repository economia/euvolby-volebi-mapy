Political parties represented in the election:

  • VA-TB/LNNK - Union For Fatherland and Freedom/LNNK (Apvieníba "Tévzemei un Brívíbai"/LNNK )
  • JL - The New Era (Jaunais laiks)
  • PCTVL - Union of Political organizations "For Human Rights in a United Latvia" (Politisko organizáciju apvieníba "Par cilvéka tiesíbám vienotá Latvijá")
  • TP - People’s Party (Tautas partija)
  • SLC - Alliance Latvia's Way (Savieníba Latvijas celš)
  • TSP - People’s Harmony Party (Tautas saskanas partija)
  • LSDSP - Latvian Social Democratic Workers Party (Latvijas Sociáldemokrátiská strádnieku partija)
  • ZZS - Union of the Green and Farmers ("Zalo un Zemnieku savieníba")
  • LPP - Latvia’s First Party ("Latvijas Pirmá Partija")
  • SDLP - Socialdemocratic Welfare Party (Sociáldemokrátiská Labklájíbas partija)
  • Other - Other parties (less than 2,0 % of total votes):
    • KP - The Conservative Party (Konservatívá partija)
    • LSP - Latvia's Socialistic Party (Latvijas Sociálistiská partija)
    • LG - Light of Latgale (Latgales Gaisma)
    • Eiroskeptiki - Political organization "Eurosceptics" (Politiská organizácija (partija) "Eiroskeptiki")
    • LKDS - Latvia's Christian Democratic Union (Latvijas Kristígo demokrátu savieníba)
    • SDS - Union of Social Democrats ("Sociáldemokrátu savieníba - SDS")
