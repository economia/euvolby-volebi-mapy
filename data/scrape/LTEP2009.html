Political parties represented in the election:

  • TS-LKD - Homeland Union - Lithuanian Christian Democrats (Tevynes sajunga - Lietuvos krikšcionys demokratai)
  • LSDP - Lithuanian Social Democratic Party (Lietuvos socialdemokratu partija)
  • TT - Party "Order and Justice" (Liberal Democratic Party) (Partija Tvarka ir teisingumas)
  • DP - Labor Party ( Darbo partija)
  • LLRA - Lithuanian Poles' Electoral Action (Lietuvos lenku rinkimu akcija)
  • LRLS - Liberals Movement of the Republic of Lithuania (Lietuvos Respublikos liberalu sajudis)
  • LCS - Liberal and Centre Union (Liberalu ir centro sajunga)
  • LCP - Lithuanian Centre Party (Lietuvos centro partija)
  • KKSS - Christian Conservatives Social Union (Krikšcioniu konservatoriu socialine sajunga)
  • FRONTAS - Political party 'FRONTAS' ("FRONTO" PARTIJA)
  • LVLS - Union of Lithuanian Peasants and Peoples (Lietuvos valstieciu liaudininku sajunga)
  • PDP - Party of Civic Democracy (Pilietines demokratijos partija)
  • ZP - Samogitian party (Žemaiciu partija)
  • TPP - Rising Nation Party (Tautos prisikelimo partija)
  • TPLK - National Party "Way of Lithuania" (Tautine partija LIETUVOS KELIAS)
