Political parties represented in the election:

  • ND - New Democracy Nea Dhimokratia
  • PASOK - Panhellenic Socialist Movement (Panellinio Sosialistiko Kinima)
  • KKE - Communist Party of Greece (Kommunistiko Komma Elladas)
  • SYN - Left Coalition (Synaspismos (Synaspismos tis aristeras kai tis proodou))
  • LA.O.S - Popular Orthodox Rally (Laks Orthdoxos Synagerms)
  • GYN.EYR - Women for Another Europe
  • DPE - Democratic Regional Union (Dimokratiki Periferiaki Enosi)
  • OIK.PRAS - Green Party of Greece (Oikologoi Prasinoi)
  • EN.KENT - Union of Centrists (Enosi Kentroon)
  • VERGHS - Greek Ecologists
  • Other parties (less than 0,5 % of total votes):
    • M-L KKE - Marxist-Leninist Communist Party of Greece (Marxistiko - Leninistiko Kommunistiko Komma Elladas)
    • ELL.MET - Hellenic Front (Elliniko Metopo)
    • DISOK - Socialist Democratic Movement
    • MERA - Radical Left Front (Metopo Rizospastikis Aristeras)
    • ANT.SYM - Anticapitalism Coalition
    • ASKE - Fighting Socialist Party (Agnostiko Sosialistiko Komma Elladas)
    • PAT.SYM - Patriotic Coalition
    • OT - Rainbow Party (Ouranio Toxo (Evropako Kinima))
    • ORAMA - Vision
    • OAKKE - Organization for the Reconstruction of the Communist Party (Organosi gia tin anasyngrotisi tou KKE)
    • EYR.SYMP - European Confederacy
    • OIK.ENAL - Ecologist Alternative (Ecologi Enallaktiki)
    • CHRIST - Belief in Jesus Christ (Christopistia)
