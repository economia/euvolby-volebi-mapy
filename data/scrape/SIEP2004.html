Political parties represented in the election:

  • LDS-DeSUS - Liberal Democracy of Slovenia and Democratic Party of Retired Persons of Slovenia (Liberalna Demokracija Slovenije in Demokraticna Stranka Upokojencev Slovenij)   
  • SDS - Slovenian Democratic Party (Slovenska Demokratska Stranka)
  • ZLSD - United List of Social Democrats (Zdruzena Lista Socialnih Demokratov)
  • SLS - Slovenian People's Party (Slovenska Ljudska Stranka)
  • NSi - New Slovenia, Christian People's Party (Nova Slovenija - Kršcanska Ljudska Stranka)
  • SMS-ZELENI - Youth Party of Slovenia and Greens of Slovenia (Stranka Mladih Slovenije in Zeleni Slovenije)
  • SNS - Slovene National Party (Slovenska Nacionalna Stranka)
  • DS - Democratic Party of Slovenia (Demokratska Stranka Slovenije - Demokrati Slovenije)
  • SJN - Slovenia is ours (Slovenija Je Naša)
  • GŽS - Voice of Slovenian women (Glas Žensk Slovenije)
  • SEG - Party of Ecological Movement of Slovenia (Stranka Ekoloških Gibanj Slovenije)
  • NSD - National Labour Party (Nacionalna Stranka Dela)
  • SSN - Party of Slovenian People (Stranka Slovenskega Naroda)
